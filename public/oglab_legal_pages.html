If you have registered or used our services through Facebook Login and wish to delete your data.

1. Go to Facebook Apps and Websites and log into your Facebook account.

2. Locate "OG Lab" in the list of active apps and click "Remove" to disconnect your account.

This step is optional but recommended if you want to ensure that all your data, including any possible cached copies, are completely and permanently removed from our systems after disconnecting your account.

We will process your request and delete all related data from our system within 30 business days.
