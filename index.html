Welcome to OG Lab

OG Lab produces cannabis products, including flowers and innovative items such as Andaman cannabis cigarettes with a patented filter that cools the smoke, terpene-infused cannabis fractions, and clones of their plants with outstanding phenotypes. We emphasize high ecological and quality standards and aim to be a leader in Thailand's cannabis industry and culture. Our team is international, works in Thailand, and values creating a positive and enjoyable work environment. We support our partners, such as cannabis shop owners, with training and opportunities to achieve success and inspiring profits. Our cannabis farm is licensed and operates legally.
